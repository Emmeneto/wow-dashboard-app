Privacy Policy

WoW Dashboard · Last Updated: April 2026

What Data We Collect

WoW Dashboard collects the following types of data from your World of Warcraft characters:

  • Character identity: character name, realm, class, specialization, level, and guild name
  • Gear data: equipped item names, item levels, item IDs, and quality
  • Progression data: weekly vault progress, dungeon/raid/world activity counts, quest completion status
  • General stats: gold, bag space, professions, mounts, pets, playtime, and current location
  • Chat interactions: messages you send to the AI advisor, along with AI responses

Why We Collect It

All data is collected for the sole purpose of providing personalized gearing recommendations for your characters. Specifically:

  • To display your character stats, gear, and weekly progress on the dashboard
  • To generate rule-based and AI-powered gearing advice tailored to your current equipment and progression
  • To track your weekly tasks and vault progress
  • To compare your equipped gear against Best-in-Slot targets

How Data Is Stored

Your data is stored server-side in JSON files on the dashboard server. Key points:

  • Data is not shared with third parties
  • Data is not sold, rented, or transferred to any external service
  • AI chat requests are sent to Anthropic's Claude API for processing, but only character context needed for the conversation is included
  • No personally identifiable information (real name, email, IP addresses) is collected through the addon

Data Retention

We retain your character data for a maximum of 90 days from the date of last upload. After 90 days of inactivity, your data is automatically deleted from the server.

Chat conversation logs are retained for a rolling window of the most recent 1,000 entries, after which older entries are automatically removed.

The Addon vs. The Companion App

The in-game WoWDashboard addon itself does NOT transmit any data externally. It only reads your character information and writes it to a local SavedVariables file on your computer.

Data is only sent to the dashboard server when you explicitly use the companion upload tool (upload.js) with your consent. The upload is initiated by you and requires an active choice to run.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data by viewing the dashboard with your user key
  • Request deletion of all your data at any time
  • Withdraw consent by declining data collection in the dashboard consent banner
  • Stop uploading at any time by simply not running the companion upload tool

To request data deletion or exercise any of your rights, please contact us using the information below.
